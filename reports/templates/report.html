04Methodology
Scoring Architecture
Two-pass evaluation: heuristic pre-screen followed by LLM-as-Judge with module-specific prompts. A finding is confirmed when judge confidence exceeds 72%. Risk scores are severity-weighted on a CVSS-aligned 0–10 scale.
Probe Generation
Each seed payload is mutated across 5 variant strategies: direct, role-play wrapper, hypothetical framing, base64 encoding, and indirect injection — testing multiple evasion angles per attack type.

05Scope & Limitations
This assessment is scoped to automated prompt-based testing using the LANCE framework v0.4.0. It does not cover infrastructure security, model training data attacks, physical access vectors, or social engineering of operators. Results represent the model's behaviour at time of testing and may vary with model updates or system prompt changes. All findings should be independently verified before remediation prioritisation.
